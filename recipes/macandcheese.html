Mac and Cheese

This is a good mac and cheese dish

This mac and cheese is very good. I made it at the good kitchen. All of the ingredients are good too. Yum, good macaroni and cheese

From the good region of Macaronia, this good mac and cheese is a some of a kind masterpiece. With macaroni from macaroni plants, and cheese from cheese plants. Yum, good mac and cheese

Ingredients

  • Sour cream
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Cheese
  • Whole milk
  • Garlic powder
  • Salted butter
  • Macaroni Noodles

Steps

  1. Boil pasta: In a large pot of salted watr, boil macaroni noodles until they are good and then drain
  2. Make: Cheese sauce, combine all cheese or milk products in good ratios, stir until good
  3. Make: A roux, stir butter and flour until good
  4. Combine: All ingredients and stir until good, and eat until it isn't
