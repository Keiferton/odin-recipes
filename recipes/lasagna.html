This lasagna is very good. I made it at the good kitchen. All of the ingredients are good too. Yum, good lasagna

From the good region of Italy, this good lasagna is a some of a kind masterpiece. With tomatoes from tomato plants, and flour from flour plants. Yum, good lasagna

Ingredients

  • Cheese Filling
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  1. Boil pasta: In a large pot of salted watr, boil lasagna noodles until they are good
  2. Prepare meat sauce: Cook meat with good stuff, and drain out bad stuff, and simmer until good
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir cheese together until good
  4. Layer and bake: meat layer, cheese layer, pasta layer, repeat until good
